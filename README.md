<h2>📊 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
This project presents an <b>AI &amp; Machine Learning Jobs Market Dashboard</b> built using <b>Power BI</b> to analyze global hiring trends, salary distribution, skill demand, and company hiring patterns.
</p>

<p>
The analysis is based on <b>5,000+ AI job postings</b> across <b>20+ companies</b>, <b>15+ technical skills</b>, and multiple global locations. The dashboard transforms raw job market data into <b>actionable insights</b> for aspiring AI professionals, recruiters, and data analysts.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Objectives</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Analyze global AI hiring trends</li>
  <li>Identify high-paying AI roles</li>
  <li>Understand in-demand skills</li>
  <li>Explore company hiring patterns</li>
  <li>Evaluate location-based salary distribution</li>
</ul>

<h2>📊 Dashboard Pages</h2>

<h3>1️⃣ Overview Dashboard</h3>
<p>High-level job market metrics:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Total job postings</li>
  <li>Average salary (~$93K)</li>
  <li>Median salary (~$102K)</li>
  <li>Remote job percentage</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Key Insight:</b> Mid-level AI professionals represent the <b>largest hiring segment</b>.</p>

<h3>2️⃣ Location Insights</h3>
<p>Analyzes geographic hiring trends.</p>
<ul>
  <li>North America offers <b>highest salaries</b></li>
  <li>Europe shows strong hiring demand</li>
  <li>India is emerging as an <b>AI talent hub</b></li>
</ul>

<p><b>Top Cities:</b> San Francisco • London • Berlin • Toronto • Bengaluru</p>

<h3>3️⃣ Company &amp; Role Insights</h3>
<p>Analyzes company hiring patterns and job roles.</p>

<p><b>Top hiring companies:</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>OpenAI</li>
  <li>Google DeepMind</li>
  <li>NVIDIA</li>
  <li>HuggingFace</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Highest paying roles:</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Deep Learning Engineer</li>
  <li>ML Engineer</li>
  <li>Computer Vision Engineer</li>
</ul>

<h3>4️⃣ Skill &amp; Hiring Insights</h3>
<p>Identifies the most in-demand AI skills.</p>

<p><b>Top Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Python</li>
  <li>Machine Learning</li>
  <li>Deep Learning</li>
  <li>NLP</li>
  <li>Cloud Computing</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Key Insight:</b> Advanced AI roles require <b>specialized deep learning skills</b>.</p>

<h2>📈 Key Insights</h2>
